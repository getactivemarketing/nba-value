What It Is
TruLine is a free AI-powered sports betting intelligence platform for MLB and NBA. It uses machine learning to analyze 28+ features per game (pitcher stats, team batting, park factors, weather), compares model predictions against odds from 11+ sportsbooks, and surfaces value bets with a transparent 0-100 Value Score. It specializes in NRFI (No Run First Inning) tracking for MLB.

The Brand
- Name: TruLine (a play on "true line" — the real line/edge vs. what the market shows)
- Tagline options: "Find the edge." / "AI finds the value. Data keeps the score." / "Every pick tracked. Every result shown."
- URL: truline.app
- Twitter: @trulineapp

Brand Personality
- Data-driven, not hype-driven. The anti-"LOCK OF THE DAY" picks account
- Transparent. Shows losses just as prominently as wins
- Confident but understated. Let the numbers speak
- Modern/technical. AI + ML + data visualization, not old-school tout energy
- Accessible. Sharp enough for analytical bettors, simple enough for casual ones

Target Audience
- Casual sports bettors (25-45, mostly male, mobile-first, follow picks on Twitter/Instagram)
- Sharp/analytical bettors who want a data-driven second opinion
- NRFI bettors specifically (growing niche in MLB betting)

Color Direction
Think: dark mode dashboard, data visualization, sports analytics. Avoid: casino/gambling red-and-gold aesthetic.
Suggested palette ideas:
- Dark navy + electric blue + white — clean, analytical, trustworthy (think Bloomberg terminal meets sports)
- Dark charcoal + green accent — "money green" but subtle, data-forward
- Deep purple/indigo + cyan — modern, AI/tech feel, stands out from typical sports blue/red
